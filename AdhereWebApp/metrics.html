According to the American Heart Association, not taking medication as directed is a common problem which leads to more complications for patients in the future.

It is highly encouraged that you can adhere to the prescription plan set up by your doctor. This allow you to set long-term health goals and thus maximize healthiness.

You may always reach out to your doctor, pharmacist or other trusted healthcare professionals to give sound advice on managing your medication. Having conversations about how a certain medicine can impact your condition is key.
